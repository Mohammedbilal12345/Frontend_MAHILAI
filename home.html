About MahilAi

MahilAi is a student-driven club dedicated to empowering women through the innovative use of Artificial Intelligence (AI). We believe that AI holds the potential to create a safer, more equitable world, especially for women. Our club focuses on developing AI-powered solutions that tackle the unique challenges faced by women, ranging from personal safety to professional opportunities. At MahilAi, we create a collaborative and inclusive environment where students can explore and apply AI technologies in meaningful ways. Whether it's through workshops, hackathons, or research initiatives, we work together to harness the power of AI for positive social impact. Our team consists of passionate individuals from diverse backgrounds, all united by the common goal of using AI to make the world a safer place for women. Through innovation, education, and hands-on experience, we strive to inspire the next generation of female leaders in tech, while also contributing to the development of AI solutions that directly improve women's lives.

Activities

Workshops

Hands-on training sessions to learn AI tools, programming languages, and innovative techniques for solving real-world problems.

Seminars

Interactive sessions with industry experts sharing insights on the latest trends and applications of AI in empowering women.

Collaborative Projects

Opportunities to collaborate on AI projects that directly address social issues faced by women globally.

Hackathons

Our hackathons bring together students, professionals, and AI enthusiasts to brainstorm and build AI-driven solutions for women's safety, education, and career growth. Participants are encouraged to think creatively and deliver impactful results in a limited timeframe. Winners receive mentorship, resources, and recognition to further their ideas.
